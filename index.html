About Me

I’m Manish Rathore, a certified Yoga Coach and Professional Gym Trainer with over 7 years of hands-on experience in transforming lives through fitness and mindfulness. My mission is to guide individuals toward a healthier, stronger, and more balanced lifestyle—physically, mentally, and spiritually. I specialize in both intensive strength training and traditional yoga practices, offering a unique blend of modern gym science and ancient yogic wisdom. Whether your goal is muscle building, weight loss, improving flexibility, or relieving stress, I tailor every program to your specific body type, fitness level, and lifestyle. My programs are designed not just to build a body—but to build discipline, focus, and inner peace. From personalized workout plans and nutrition coaching to guided meditation and pranayama breathing techniques, I ensure my clients experience a complete transformation—inside and out.
